Manali

Manali is a beautiful hill station in Himachal Pradesh, known for its cold climate and scenic landscapes.

  1. Lake - A serene water body.
  2. Hilly Area - Surrounded by lush green hills.
  3. Glaciers - Stunning ice formations.
  4. Mountains - Perfect for trekking and adventure.
  5. People - Friendly and welcoming locals.
  • Banana - Fresh and delicious.
  • Sandwich - A must-try street food.
  • Paratha - Traditional North Indian bread.
  • Dosa - South Indian crispy delight.
  • Idli - Soft and fluffy rice cakes.
